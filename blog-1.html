The Spark

My fascination with technology began at age 12 when I first disassembled an old radio. The intricate circuitry and the way components communicated fascinated me beyond words.

This curiosity evolved into a passion when I built my first computer at 15, learning through trial and error how hardware and software interact to create the digital experiences we take for granted.

What truly cemented my path was realizing technology's potential to solve real-world problems at scale. The ability to create something from nothing using just logic and creativity felt like magic.
